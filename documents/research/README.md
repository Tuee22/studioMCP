# Research Folder Guide

**Status**: Reference only
**Supersedes**: N/A
**Referenced by**: [../README.md](../README.md#studiomcp-documentation-index), [../../STUDIOMCP_DEVELOPMENT_PLAN.md](../../STUDIOMCP_DEVELOPMENT_PLAN.md#documentation-governance)

> **Purpose**: Explain how `documents/research/` is used in this repository and make clear that research notes are source material, not governed architectural or product contracts.
> **📖 Authoritative Reference**: [Documentation Standards](../documentation_standards.md#4-required-header-metadata)

Documents in this folder are stand-alone research documents. They are not authoritative for current repository behavior, APIs, architecture, or operations. Treat them as source material that may inform future development plans or Single Source of Truth documents, not as the documents that define the current system.

When research findings become normative for the repository, promote that content into the governed documentation suite under the appropriate `documents/` category and update cross-references there.
